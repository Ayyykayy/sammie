Dear Samrat ♡

Happy Birthday my love.

I don’t even know how to put everything I feel for you into words, but I wanted to leave a little piece of my heart here for you.

Sometimes I wish I could just pause everything and be right next to you, laughing with you, annoying you, holding your hand, and doing all those tiny everyday things together. Until that day comes, I hope this little garden reminds you that no matter where I am, a part of me is always with you.

Every flower here is for a little thing I love about you.

For your kindness.

For the way you care.

For the way you make me feel loved.

For being my comfort place.

You came into my life and somehow made ordinary days feel special. I love the way you love me, the way you make me smile, and the way you make me feel like I have someone who truly chooses me.

I hope you always remember how special you are, not just to me, but as the person you are. I hope you achieve everything you dream of, and I hope life gives you all the happiness you deserve.

One day, I hope we won’t have to say “I miss you” so much. One day, I hope we have our little home, our silly moments, our flowers, our peaceful mornings, and everything we keep dreaming about.

But until then, keep this tiny garden with you.

Whenever you miss me, come back here and remember...

Someone loves you more than words on a screen could ever explain.

Happy Birthday, Samrat ♡
—your person ♡
